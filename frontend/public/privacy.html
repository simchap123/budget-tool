Privacy Policy

Budget Tool · Last updated 21 July 2026

Budget Tool ("the app", "we") is a personal budgeting application operated by an individual sole operator. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, who it is shared with, and the choices you have. Questions: spentelnik@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Account information — the email address and password you use to sign in.
  • Bank account data — when you link a bank through Plaid, we receive your account names, balances, and transactions. We never receive your bank login credentials; those are handled by Plaid.
  • Imported transactions — transactions you import by CSV file or by forwarding bank/credit-card alert emails to your private import address.
  • Content you create — categories, budgets, vendors, giving, and debt/goal records you set up in the app.

How we use your information

Your data is used solely to provide the budgeting features you request: importing and categorizing transactions, tracking budgets, giving, and debt payoff, and generating reports. We do not sell your data, share it for advertising, or use it for any purpose unrelated to operating your personal budget.

Service providers we share data with

  • Plaid Inc. — to connect your bank accounts and retrieve transactions, subject to Plaid's privacy policy.
  • Google (Gemini API) — transaction descriptions may be sent to Google's Gemini API to suggest a spending category. Only the transaction description is sent, not your identity or account numbers.
  • Cloudflare — DNS, TLS, and inbound email routing for the email-import feature.
  • DigitalOcean — the server that hosts the application and database.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in a self-hosted database on a private DigitalOcean server. All traffic to and from the app is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS. Access to the server and database is restricted to the operator using strong authentication.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data only for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing spentelnik@gmail.com; we will delete it within 30 days. If you disconnect a bank, your existing transactions remain until you delete them or your account. For more detail see our data retention and deletion policy.

Your choices

  • Disconnect a linked bank at any time from Settings.
  • Pause or rotate your private email-import address at any time.
  • Request a copy of your data, or its deletion, by email.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly. Material changes will be communicated in the app or by email.
